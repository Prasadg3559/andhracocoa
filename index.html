Sustainable Cocoa from Andhra Pradesh

AndhraCocoa partners with smallholder farmers to grow, ferment, and source high‑quality cocoa beans—supporting livelihoods while protecting the land. From bean to bar, we champion transparency, fair practices, and delicious results.

Who we are

We are a cocoa‑first company headquartered in Eluru (A.P.), focused on building a resilient & ethical cocoa ecosystem in India. Our team works shoulder‑to‑shoulder with growers on best‑in‑class fermentation, drying, and quality protocols.

  • Farmer training & field support
  • Transparent pricing & traceability
  • Single‑origin lots & careful fermentation
  • Responsible procurement & zero child labour

Fair & Transparent Sourcing

We believe great chocolate starts on the farm. Our agronomy partners provide hands‑on guidance for pruning, shade management, and post‑harvest practices. Beans are graded, fermented in micro‑lots, and dried gently to preserve nuanced flavour.
